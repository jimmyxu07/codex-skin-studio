# CodexSkin Themed Template Brief — benchmark-style reset

Date: 2026-07-22
Owner: J少
Status: P0 / replaces pure-color skin attempts

## 0. Core correction

Do not make another pure-color theme.

Users are not searching for a plain dark theme or plain light theme. They want a Codex Desktop skin that expresses identity and fandom:

- idol / star mood;
- anime-style character world;
- sports / World Cup / national team energy;
- basketball/football hero atmosphere;
- esports room;
- pet/companion;
- fantasy guild / mecha cockpit.

The benchmark site leads with personal photo, pop culture, portrait, playful, gallery, remix/studio. It does not lead with plain color themes because color alone does not create ownership desire.

## 1. Product rule

Readability is a constraint, not the product hook.

The product hook must be:

```text
I can make Codex look like my world / my fandom / my team / my character / my stage.
```

## 2. Compliance model

Public templates may imitate category mechanics, not copyrighted subject matter.

Allowed:

- original fictional idol/stage character;
- original anime-style school/cyber/fantasy workspace;
- generic sports arena with non-real team colors;
- abstract national color energy without flags/crests/FIFA marks;
- original jersey pattern vibes;
- user-upload builder for private use.

Not allowed in public shipped templates:

- real celebrity likeness;
- real anime/game characters/costumes/logos;
- real player likeness;
- club/national team crests;
- FIFA/World Cup official names/marks as artwork;
- competitor images.

Builder can support user-upload, but public copy must say:

```text
Use images you own or have rights to. Do not redistribute copyrighted celebrity, anime, sports team, or player artwork without permission.
```
